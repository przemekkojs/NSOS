# Creating an Account

## Overview

This guide explains how to create an administrator account in the system.

## Requirements

- Valid email address
- Access to email for OTP verification

## Steps

1. **Navigate to Registration**
   - Go to the register page

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - Provide your email address
   - Create a password that meets security requirements

3. **Password Validation**
   - The system will verify your password meets strength requirements
   - Ensure it contains required characters and length

4. **Verify Identity**
   - Check your email for the one-time password (OTP)
   - Enter the OTP code to confirm your identity

5. **Sign In**
   - Use your new credentials to access the system

Your administrator account is now active and ready to use.
